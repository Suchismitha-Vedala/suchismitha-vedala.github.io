AI Economics

Cost behavior is an architectural property.

At enterprise scale, model choice, prompt shape, context length, cache reuse, routing policy, and evaluation thresholds decide whether an AI product can be adopted widely. Economics is designed into the platform.

Route with intent

Match tasks to models using classification, model scoring, quality bars, latency needs, and cost profiles.

Compress the right context

Shape prompts and retrieval payloads so the model sees what matters without carrying unnecessary tokens.

Reuse generation work

Use caching and semantic reuse patterns where stable answers, shared context, and repeated workflows permit it.

Qualify before expansion

Connect evaluation, observability, feedback, and product readiness before taking a capability broadly.
