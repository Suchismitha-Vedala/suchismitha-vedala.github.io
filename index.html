About

Interactive data models, not static reports

I'm a UH Cougar with a Master's in Computer Science, majoring in Data Analytics. My passion for data pulled me toward research, and my work sits where data analytics, machine learning, and artificial intelligence meet.

Because I also came up through application development and front-end work, I care about the last mile: the chart, the interface, the thing a person actually reads. My internship at American International Group taught me the software development process end to end, and my time as a research assistant at the University of Houston made me fluent with the Google, Microsoft, and YouTube Analytics APIs. Alongside my degree I hold a Udacity Data Analyst Nanodegree, because I think learning is the whole job.

I also believe work-life balance is what makes the work good. Named for a pure smile, I travel and chase new places whenever I can — and I'll happily debug for days and still be cheerful about it.
